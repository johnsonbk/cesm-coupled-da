CESM Coupled Data Assimilation
==============================

This documents our effort to configure CESM to run in various configurations to
test concepts relevant to coupled data assimilation, including:

- forced ocean-ice experiments, and
- CESM B-compsets with active CAM6, CLM, CICE6 and POP2 components.
